## Maintainer Responsibilities

All maintainers are expected to:

- **Review PRs** within 7 days of submission
- **Triage issues** at least weekly (label, assign, close duplicates)
- **Respond to security reports** within 48 hours (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md))
- **Follow the governance process** defined in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md)
- **Maintain code quality** by enforcing style guidelines and review standards
- **Be respectful and constructive** in all interactions (see [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md))
- **Stay active** — maintainers inactive for 6+ months may be moved to emeritus status

## Owner (Commander) Additional Responsibilities

The Owner has all maintainer responsibilities plus:

- Final approval on all `main` branch merges
- Repository settings and access control
- Release management and version tagging
- Governance document amendments
- Security policy decisions
- Maintainer appointment and removal

## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainer status is earned through consistent, high-quality contributions. See the [Contributor Tiers](GOVERNANCE.md#contributor-tiers) in GOVERNANCE.md for the full progression path.

**In short:**
1. Start as a Contributor (Tier 1) — submit PRs
2. Become a Trusted Contributor (Tier 2) — 3+ merged PRs, consistent quality
3. Become a Reviewer (Tier 3) — invited by existing maintainer, 10+ quality reviews
4. Become a Maintainer (Tier 4) — long-term trust, deep framework knowledge, Commander approval
