# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in RoundTable Framework a respectful, professional, and inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Providing helpful and constructive code reviews
- Acknowledging and crediting the work of others

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory remarks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spam, off-topic self-promotion, or disruptive behavior
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a workplace

## AI Contributions Clause

RoundTable Framework is an AI governance tool and welcomes AI-generated contributions. AI-assisted and AI-generated work is held to the **same standards** as human contributions. AI-generated content must be clearly disclosed. The human sponsor of an AI-generated PR bears full responsibility for its content and quality.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:
- GitHub repository (issues, PRs, discussions, wiki)
- Official communication channels
- Any public space where an individual represents the project

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at the contact below. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.

### Enforcement Actions

1. **Correction** — A private written warning with clarity on the nature of the violation
2. **Warning** — A formal warning with consequences for continued behavior
3. **Temporary Ban** — Temporary restriction from community interaction
4. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent removal from all community spaces
